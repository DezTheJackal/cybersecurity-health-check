What's Covered

Five Areas That Keep You Safe

Each area is assessed against real-world attack patterns, not just a checklist.

01

Password and Account Security

We review your password policies, shared credentials, default accounts, and privilege exposure across staff accounts and critical systems.

02

Multi-Factor Authentication

MFA is your most effective defence against account takeover. We check what is enabled, what is missing, and where your gaps are most dangerous.

03

Backup and Recovery

Ransomware only wins if you have no way back. We assess your backup coverage, frequency, offsite copies, and whether recovery has ever actually been tested.

04

Exposed Services Review

We identify services and ports that are unnecessarily exposed to the internet. Remote desktop, file sharing, and management interfaces are common entry points.

05

Written Findings Report

You receive a clear, jargon-free report with every identified risk, its potential impact on your business, and concrete steps to fix it. No vague recommendations.
